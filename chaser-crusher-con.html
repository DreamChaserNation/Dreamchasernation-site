A personal note from Paul

I’ve always loved hosting events. Friends and family have seen Graham Bash evolve over the years — what began as a backyard BBQ nearly 20 years ago has grown into 16 Graham Bashes, 2 Graham Slashes (Halloween‑style), and counting. There’s just something about the energy of bringing everyone together for one fun‑filled event each year.

My dream now is to channel that same energy into Dream Chaser Nation — to make Chase & Crush Con a reality. If you’re interested in attending, I want to hear from you.
