About Us

Dream Chaser Nation was born from a belief that life’s work should fuel life—not steal from it. We’re builders, learners, and believers who want to move from ideas to action, one day at a time. Our mission is simple: help people chase the dreams that give their life meaning—and do it in a way that creates space for what matters most. We believe success isn’t just about achievement; it’s about freedom, purpose, and the people you share it with.

Family & Mission

Family is the heartbeat of Dream Chaser Nation. It’s the reason we started, the compass that keeps us grounded, and the reward that makes the chase worth it. For me, this began with a simple truth—nothing is more important than a family day. The chance to unplug, be present, and make memories that last. My wife and I fought hard to build our family, and that journey made us appreciate every moment even more.

That experience fuels our mission: to help others design a life where they can chase their dreams without losing the moments that matter. It’s why we’re creating Lil Dream Chaser U—a place where kids can imagine boldly, learn what schools don’t always teach, and see that life’s biggest wins start with the right mindset.

Because dreams don’t happen by accident—they grow from habits, hope, and heart. And when you surround yourself with other dreamers who believe in building their lives on their terms, the impossible starts to look like the next milestone.

As part of our mission, we’re building Lil Dream Chaser U — a free learning space for children.
